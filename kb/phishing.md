# Phishing Playbook

Phishing is a form of social engineering where attackers deceive people into revealing sensitive information or installing malware.

## Recommended Remediation
1. **Quarantine Email**: Remove the phishing email from all users' inboxes.
2. **Scan Devices**: Run a full antivirus scan on the devices of users who interacted with the email.
3. **Reset Credentials**: If a user provided their password, force an immediate reset and terminate active sessions.
4. **Security Training**: Enlist the affected users in additional security awareness training.
